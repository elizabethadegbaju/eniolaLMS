Frequently Asked Questions

How many books can I request at a time?

You can reserve as many books as you want but all reserved books must be collected within 24 hours. Also, note that you cannot collect more than one book from the library at any point in time.

What can I do if a book I need is not available?

There are a wide variety of books available for you in the library. You can use the search option to browse for books that are similar or you can check back on the site daily to see when there is a copy available for you to pick up.

Where do I return a book I borrowed?

You are to take the book back to the library and hand it over to the librarian. You can check your online profile before you leave the library to make sure it has been updated accordingly.

Where can I check out books?

You can checkout books physically in the library with or without reservations so long as they are available.

Can I renew books?

Online renewals of collected books are not allowed. You have to take the book back to the library before the due date for it to be checked in. Inform the librarian that you are there for a book renewal and they will reset the clock for the aforementioned book.
